# Claims And Non-Claims

## This Artifact Supports

- Review-evidence availability measurement within AIDev-pop.
- Handoff-evidence gap measurement.
- Provenance-backed auditability.
- Delayed-repeat self-consistency.
- Offline reproducibility of schema validation, CLI smoke workflows, perturbation checks, dossier cards, and paper-facing derived tables.

## This Artifact Does Not Support

- Patch correctness.
- Mergeability.
- Reviewer utility.
- All-GitHub rates.
- AI-vs-human causal effects.
- Inter-rater reliability.

## Wording Boundary

Use: "review-evidence availability", "handoff-evidence gap", "provenance-backed evidence", "single-operator audit", and "delayed-repeat self-consistency".

Avoid: "correctness benchmark", "mergeability benchmark", "reviewer utility proven", "AI PRs are worse than human PRs", "all GitHub", and "inter-rater reliability established".
