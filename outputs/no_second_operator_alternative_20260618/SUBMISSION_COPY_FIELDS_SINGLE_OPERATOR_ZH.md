## Artifact / Reproducibility Note

The anonymous artifact package is available for review at:

https://github.com/whatareyoulaughing-cyber/mergedossier-bench-artifact/releases/tag/v0.1-anonymous

The package includes source code, schemas, safe examples, derived population tables, provenance-audit outputs, perturbation fixtures, smoke-test instructions, and an anonymization scan report. DOI archival is planned after double-anonymous constraints are lifted or if the venue explicitly permits anonymous DOI archival.

## Audit Boundary

The AIDev-pop audit was conducted by one operator with 50 delayed repeats. We report delayed-repeat self-consistency, provenance-backed inspectability, perturbation checks, sensitivity analyses, and handoff-evidence-gap robustness rather than inter-rater reliability. A deterministic 50-task external-audit packet is included for future independent replication, but no external agreement is claimed in the submitted results.

## Non-Claims

The paper does not claim patch correctness, mergeability, reviewer utility, AI-vs-human causal effects, all-GitHub population rates, inter-rater reliability, or DOI archival during double-anonymous review.
