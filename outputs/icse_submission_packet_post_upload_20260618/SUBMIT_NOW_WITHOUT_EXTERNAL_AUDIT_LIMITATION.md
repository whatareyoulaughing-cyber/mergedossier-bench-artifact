## Limitation Paragraph

The AIDev-pop audit was conducted by a single operator. To reduce protocol drift, the study uses fixed audit-code definitions, provenance-backed dossier cards, schema validation, perturbation checks, and 50 delayed-repeat records. These checks support instrument auditability and single-operator self-consistency, but they do not establish inter-rater reliability. We therefore phrase the empirical result as a bounded evidence-availability profile within the AIDev-pop frame, not as a validated subjective agreement result or a claim about reviewer utility.
